Keep leads out of Zoho with Clio

Step one involves ion8 configuring Zoho CRM (Customer Relationship Management). Zoho captures leads from your website. Zoho also captures emails and, if you have the right phone system, your phone calls; automatically. The history of all communications with any lead or contact can be found all in one place; their contact record inside Zoho CRM. ion8 will also configure your Clio to automatically capture all emails, and phone calls too.

Automatic creation of Matters in Clio

In step two, ion8 will set it up so that when you close business with a prospect (i.e receive a retainer), all biographical and account information stored inside Zoho will automatically populate Clio and launch an according Matter assigned to the correct attorney. If you’ve also chosen to take advantage of our electronic document assembly service, ion8 will semi-automate the creation of your documents and automatically attach completed documents to the correct Matter inside Clio. This reduces churn on each file for both you and your staff.

Clean up Clio with our Pipeline for Practice Management Solution

If you’ve been entering leads for your practice straight into Clio, chances are you have a batch of useless records that you’ll never use. Or worse, you could be using an automated pipeline which brings in leads from your website which leaves Clio populated with partial or useless data.

With our solution, all leads go into a customer relationship management (CRM) program called Zoho, and automatically populate to Clio only when you receive a retainer from the client - all with a minimum amount of involvement from you. Clio stays reserved for actual clients, with all the information you need being populated to new matters.

Make More Money with Automated Client Intake & Document Assembly

Technology should help us automate repetitive tasks. We’ve done that with a solution that lets your firm automate client intake and document assembly in a way that feels personal to each of your clients – without the usual hours of work to assemble case files. With this solution in place, all documents are written and assembled automatically, making it look like you’ve spent 20 hours on a case file, when in fact you’ve spent around 2.

How our solution works:

  • 1) Initial Launch Meeting

    After agreeing to hire your firm, your new client’s contact record is created in Clio, along with the accompanying Matter. Your initial consultation is scheduled.

  • 2) Post-Consultation

    After meeting with the client, the solution will send out a link to a personalized form to capture additional information needed in the creation of the client docket. Your firm will be able to control the cadence of these emails to mirror the sense of urgency the client asked for. This will give the client the perception that the attorney is working as quickly as they requested.

  • 3) Automatic Document Assembly

    Once the client has answered all pertinent information, the correct documents are created automatically, and are sent to your firm for review before they are sent to the client to sign electronically.

  • 4) Secure Electronic Signatures

    PandaDoc, the solution we use for this piece, allows you to accept electronic signatures securely, offering full encryption and timestamps which show exactly where and when a document was signed.
